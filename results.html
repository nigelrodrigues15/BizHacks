Industry Insights

The yoga mat industry ​​analysis shows the evolving nature of fitness all over the world. Characterized by embracing the customer experience through product, driving brand engagement, dominating delivery, and competing with non-traditional players, this industry is only to grow. Today’s customers expect a high-quality product every time, and across every channel. Winning yoga mat providers will be the ones who invest wisely in digital, operations, marketing, and technology, and can harness the power of their delivery to serve as brand ambassadors at the moments that matter. The next-generation customer is technologically savvy, connected, and has high expectations. Yoga mat brands who understand their customers, capitalize on digital/technology investment sand analytics, and seize upon the opportunity to engage customers in a highly personalized way can drive increased transaction frequency, customer conversion, and loyalty.

Market Insight

In the yoga mat e-commerce business, consumers are more conscious than ever about making responsible selections and increasingly want to make sure they are purchasing high quality fitness equipment. The market demands higher quality products and they are more open to paying more for quality more than ever. For every 1000 units of yoga mats sold in e-commerce businesses, 80% are in the upper 20 percentile in terms of costs. This means that a large percentage of yoga mat users are prioritizing more of a higher quality product for their health.
